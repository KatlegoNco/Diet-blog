Vegan Diet

Recipes

1. Vegan Buddha Bowl

  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 cup roasted chickpeas
  • Mixed veggies and tahini dressing
Method:

Assemble all ingredients in a bowl. Drizzle with tahini dressing and enjoy.

2. Vegan Stir Fry

  • 1 block tofu
  • Mixed bell peppers
  • Soy sauce, garlic, and ginger
Method:

Stir-fry tofu and veggies with seasoning for 7-10 minutes. Serve hot.
