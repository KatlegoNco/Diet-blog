Intermittent Fasting Diet

Recipes

1. Fasting Friendly Smoothie

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp honey
Method:

Blend all ingredients until smooth. Drink during your eating window.

2. Grilled Chicken Salad

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil & lemon dressing
Method:

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Toss well before serving.
