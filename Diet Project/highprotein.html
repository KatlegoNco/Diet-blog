High-Protein Diet

Recipes

1. Protein-Packed Omelette

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup shredded chicken breast
Method:

Beat eggs and pour into a pan. Add the vegetables and chicken. Cook until firm and serve hot.

2. Grilled Steak with Veggies

  • 1 beef steak (200g)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup steamed broccoli
  • 1/2 cup grilled zucchini
Method:

Season steak with salt and pepper. Grill to desired doneness. Serve with steamed broccoli and grilled zucchini.

3. Grilled Chicken Breast with Quinoa

  • 2 chicken breasts (200g)
  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup steamed broccoli
Method:

Season chicken with salt and pepper. Grill to desired doneness. Serve with steamed broccoli and quinoa.
